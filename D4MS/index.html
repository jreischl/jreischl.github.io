What is MS?

MS stops people from moving. We exist to make sure it doesn’t. The National MS Society addresses challenges of each person affected by MS by funding cutting edge research, driving change through advocacy, facilitating professional education, collaborating with MS organizations around the world, and providing programs and services designed to help people with MS and their families move their lives forward. We are people who want to do something about MS NOW, and are dedicated to achieving a world free of MS. Join the movement at nationalMSsociety.org.
